FAQ

Common participant questions

How do I join a study?

Open the secure study link or scan the QR code provided by your research team, then follow the consent and permission steps in the app.

Why does the app ask for location or Screen Time permission?

StudyTrace requests permissions only for features that may be used by a study. Screen Time permission is optional and is used only when the study protocol asks for selected-app, website, or category usage summaries.

Does StudyTrace collect Screen Time for every app?

No. StudyTrace asks you to select the specific apps, websites, or app categories that may be included. StudyTrace does not collect Screen Time data for apps you do not select. Some studies need selected-app summaries because broad categories are too coarse for the approved research question.

Why might StudyTrace ask me to label selected apps?

Apple may limit the app identifiers available outside the Screen Time report. If your study uses selected-app summaries, StudyTrace may ask you to provide labels such as “Messaging” or “Instagram” so the research dashboard can interpret the summaries you chose to share.

Can I stop participating?

Yes. You can quit the study from the app settings. For questions about previously uploaded data or formal withdrawal under the study protocol, contact the study coordinator.

Why am I not receiving survey reminders?

Check that notifications are enabled for StudyTrace in iPhone Settings and confirm with the research team that your study has active survey schedules.

What if the app does not upload or seems inactive?

Reopen the app, verify that the study is still joined, and confirm permissions are still enabled. If the problem continues, contact your study coordinator with the approximate time of the issue.
